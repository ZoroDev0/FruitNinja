About Fruit Slice Arena

Fruit Slice Arena is a fast-paced, browser-based arcade game where you swipe your blade through flying fruits to score points. The game runs entirely in your browser — no downloads, no accounts, no tracking. Just pure slicing action.

How It Works

Fruits fly across the canvas and you click-and-drag to slice them before they disappear. Chain multiple slices for combo bonuses, avoid missing fruits (each miss costs a life), and try to rack up the highest score within the 60-second timer.

Your Data

Your game scores and leaderboard entries are synced globally via Firebase so all players can see and compete. Community posts are also shared globally. Admin controls remain local to your browser.
